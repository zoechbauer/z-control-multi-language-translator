The translation is done via the Google Translate API in the cloud. The translated texts are neither stored nor shared.

We store the following anonymous data in Google Firebase/Firestore:

  • Device information (platform type)
  • Number of translated characters
  • Selected target languages
  • Date of last translation
  • Anonymized user ID
This data is used solely to manage monthly usage quotas and prevent unexpected costs. No personal or sensitive data is stored.

The app does not use any external services or libraries that share data with third parties. Your data remains private and secure on your device.

You can find the complete privacy policy on the z-control website Privacy Policy
